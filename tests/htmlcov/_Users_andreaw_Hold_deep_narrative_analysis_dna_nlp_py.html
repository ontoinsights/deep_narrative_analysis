93def _get_named_entities_in_sentence(nlp_sentence: Doc, sentence_dict: dict): 

94 """

98 :param nlp_sentence: The sentence (a spaCy Doc) 

99 :param sentence_dict: A dictionary that is updated with time-, location- and/or event-related details 

100 :return: None (sentence_dict is updated with the text of the named entities, where the 

101 key is either 'LOCS' or 'TIMES')
